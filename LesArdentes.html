LesArdentes

This project marks a significant milestone as my inaugural solo endeavor, delving into the realm of PHP for the first time. The primary objective was to construct a booking site centered around my passion for music. The site incorporates functionality for information retrieval, email communication, and dynamic sorting of various elements based on day, date, or alphabetically. Harnessing the power of HTML, CSS, JS, and PHP, I navigated the intricacies of web development to bring this music-themed booking site to life, enriching my skill set along the way.
